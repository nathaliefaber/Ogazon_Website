OGAZÓN

    BIO

    Ogazón is an artist that truly embodies the spirit of the music she plays. Releasing all inhibitions, she pours herself totally into her performances. Her approach is one of careful consideration, with the audience's journey at its heart. Deeply sensitive to the subtle and contextual nature of electronic music, she is equally adept at presenting both house and techno, often rendering the perceived boundary between non-existent.

    With forward-facing music that propels, she's able to make creative progress in an already well-established art form while remaining true to its fundamentals -the mark of a student truly passionate about their subject matter.
